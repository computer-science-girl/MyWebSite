Baton Twirling

This image depicts baton twirling, a sport of which I have been passionately involved in since the age of 11. I possess the ability to twirl one, two, and three batons. Throughout my baton career, I have served as a majorette, traveled to competitions, and partaken in twirling clinics with the West Virginia University Feature Twirlers. Now, in addition to twirling individually, I give lessons to aspiring majorettes and have coreographed solos for majorettes to perform.
